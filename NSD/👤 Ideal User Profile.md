## ANALYZE

- This is **not a mass-market product**
    
- It serves **decision-makers under uncertainty**
    
- Value = **speed + clarity → action**

- Operator, Allocator, Strategist are already implied
    

---

## 👤 3 Ideal User Profiles

---

# 👤 1. The Operator (Builder / Executor)

### 🎯 Motivation

- “What should I build or pivot into _right now_?”
    
- Feels pressure to move fast but hates guessing
    

### ⚙️ Behaviors

- Constantly scanning markets, trends, competitors
    
- Reacts late because signal is unclear
    
- Makes bets based on incomplete information
    

### ✅ Needs (Win Condition)

- Clear **business opportunities tied to real money movement**
    
- Specific **execution paths** (not ideas)
    
- Confidence that this isn’t “just another trend”
    

### 🧠 Reality

- Will pay if it leads to **revenue within months**
    
- Rejects anything that feels like “research” instead of direction
    

---

# 👤 2. The Capital Allocator (Investor / Fund Manager)

### 🎯 Motivation

- “Where should capital go _before the market prices it in_?”
    

### ⚙️ Behaviors

- Uses reports, analysts, and networks
    
- Often late because information is consensus-driven
    
- Spends time filtering noise vs signal
    

### ✅ Needs (Win Condition)

- Early signals of **policy-driven capital flows**
    
- Quantifiable **upside + risk clarity**
    
- Ability to justify decisions to partners or LPs
    

### 🧠 Reality

- Doesn’t care about “ideas”
    
- Cares about:
    
    - timing
        
    - positioning
        
    - asymmetric upside
        

---

# 👤 3. The Policy Strategist (Translator / Influence Layer)

### 🎯 Motivation

- “How does this policy actually change the real world?”
    

### ⚙️ Behaviors

- Reads policy deeply
    
- Writes reports, briefs, or advises orgs
    
- Struggles to connect policy → business outcomes
    

### ✅ Needs (Win Condition)

- Clear mapping from **policy → economic impact**
    
- Structured outputs that are **defensible and explainable**
    
- Ability to communicate impact to stakeholders
    

### 🧠 Reality

- Values **credibility + traceability**
    
- Needs:
    
    - source links
        
    - confidence levels
        
    - logic chains
