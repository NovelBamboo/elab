## DRAFT 👤 3 Ideal User Profiles (ELaB)

---

## **1. Primary User — Independent Operator / Founder**

### **Motivations**

- Looking for **new business ideas or pivots**
    
- Wants to enter markets **before they become obvious**
    
- Feels current idea sourcing is saturated or random
    

### **Behaviors**

- Consumes newsletters, X threads, industry reports
    
- Experiments with ideas quickly (low attachment)
    
- Watches trends but struggles to connect them to **real opportunities**
    

### **Needs (Win Conditions)**

- Clear signal: _“This law creates this business opportunity”_
    
- Fast validation: _Is this worth pursuing?_
    
- Direction: _What’s the simplest way to start?_
    

👉 **Win = leaves with 1 actionable business idea tied to a law**

---

## **2. Secondary User — Small Fund / Independent Investor**

### **Motivations**

- Wants **asymmetric bets** based on early signals
    
- Seeks structured ways to track **emerging markets**
    
- Needs faster insight without building a research team
    

### **Behaviors**

- Reviews deals, trends, and market shifts regularly
    
- Relies on fragmented sources (news, analysts, network)
    
- Often reacts after narratives are already forming
    

### **Needs (Win Conditions)**

- Early identification of **law-driven opportunities**
    
- Clear mapping: _who benefits, who loses_
    
- Confidence in signal (not speculation)
    

👉 **Win = identifies investable opportunity before market consensus**

---

## **3. Tertiary User — Operator Inside Existing Business**

### **Motivations**

- Needs to **respond to regulatory changes quickly**
    
- Wants to avoid being disrupted or missing upside
    
- Responsible for adapting strategy under uncertainty
    

### **Behaviors**

- Waits for internal/legal summaries
    
- Reacts after changes are already impacting operations
    
- Relies on consultants or delayed analysis
    

### **Needs (Win Conditions)**

- Immediate clarity: _What changed and why it matters_
    
- Strategic direction: _What should the business do now_
    
- Reduced ambiguity → faster internal decisions
    

👉 **Win = makes a faster, more informed strategic adjustment than competitors**
