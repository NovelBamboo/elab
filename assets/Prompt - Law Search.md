In December 2024, the 118th Congress enacted several significant laws:

1. **Financial Management Risk Reduction Act**: This act amends section 7504 of title 31, United States Code, to enhance single audit requirements. It was signed into law on December 23, 2024. ([congress.gov](https://www.congress.gov/118/plaws/publ207/PLAW-118publ207.pdf?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Public Law 118–207 118th Congress An Act"))
    
2. **Improving Federal Building Security Act of 2024**: Signed into law on December 17, 2024, this act mandates that Facility Security Committees respond to security recommendations issued by the Federal Protective Service concerning facility security. ([congress.gov](https://www.congress.gov/118/plaws/publ157/PLAW-118publ157.pdf?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Public Law 118–157 118th Congress An Act"))
    
3. **Public Law 118–162**: Enacted on December 23, 2024, this law clarifies jurisdiction regarding certain Bureau of Reclamation pumped storage development. ([congress.gov](https://www.congress.gov/118/plaws/publ162/PLAW-118publ162.pdf?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Public Law 118–162 118th Congress An Act"))
    
4. **Public Law 118–204**: Signed into law on December 23, 2024, this act designates the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 180 Steuart Street in San Francisco, California, as the "Harvey Milk Post Office Building." ([congress.gov](https://www.congress.gov/118/plaws/publ204/PLAW-118publ204.pdf?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Public Law 118–204 118th Congress An Act"))
    
5. **National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2025**: This comprehensive defense policy bill authorizes appropriations for military activities of the Department of Defense for fiscal year 2025. It was signed into law on December 23, 2024. ([en.wikipedia.org](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Defense_Authorization_Act_for_Fiscal_Year_2025?utm_source=chatgpt.com "National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2025"))
    
6. **Social Security Fairness Act**: Signed into law on January 5, 2025, this act repeals the Government Pension Offset and Windfall Elimination Provision, affecting Social Security benefits for certain public sector retirees. ([en.wikipedia.org](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Social_Security_Fairness_Act?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Social Security Fairness Act"))
    
7. **Tax Relief for American Families and Workers Act**: This tax legislation amends portions of the Internal Revenue Code, including expanding the Child Tax Credit and restoring certain business tax breaks. It was approved by the House on January 31, 2024, and is expected to be included in the upcoming reconciliation package in the 119th Congress. ([en.wikipedia.org](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tax_Relief_for_American_Families_and_Workers_Act?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Tax Relief for American Families and Workers Act"))
